May 1-3, 2026

Rainbow Lodge, North Bend, Washington

Welcome to the Rainbow Lodge in North Bend!

The venue is a beautiful cedar frame building set on 40 acres of forest, right at the base of Mount Si. You'll enjoy forested trails, lawn games, bonfires, and more over the course of the weekend.

While the wedding ceremony will be on Saturday, all guests are invited to join Friday and Sunday activities as well. This is optional, but we'd love to have you there :)

Bedrooms at the lodge are available for $110/night. Most meals are complementary, but a couple are a la carte ($17 each). Specifics can be found on the RSVP page.
